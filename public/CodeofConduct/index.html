Rezzanine Code of Conduct

Each patron of the Rezzanine will:

  • Act in an honest and ethical manner.
  • Respect the physical and emotional well-being and the dignity of teammates, competitors, staff, and patrons.
  • Respect all public and personal property and equipment belonging to the Rezzanine and Phoenix Theater. This includes keeping food and drink away from gaming equipment.
  • Respect the policies and rules of the Phoenix theater as well as all of the staff and patrons present. This includes refraining from excessive shouting and noise that may disturb the movie watchers below.
  • Compete in a manner that is consistent with the principles of fair play, rules of competition, and honesty.
  • Promote, through attitude and behavior, a positive image of the Rezzanine and eSports in general. Be aware that this establishment is all-inclusive and intended for people and families of all ages. Foul language, shouting, and rude behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Be respectful of the staff and abide by any rules or requests they may make.
