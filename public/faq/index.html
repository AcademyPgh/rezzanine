Frequently asked questions.

1. What is the Rezzanine?

The Rezzanine is a state of the art gaming facility located at the Chartiers Valley Shopping center in Bridgeville, PA. The Rezzanine resides on the 2nd floor of the Phoenix Theatres Luxury 14 movie theatre. Whether you’re a father who needs something for the entire family to enjoy, or a hardcore eSports competitor, there is something for everyone. The Rezzanine offers a wide variety of console and PC games as well as classic games, pinball, board games, and more. There is also plenty of room to host a party, stream to your loyal followers, grab a bite to eat, or just sit down and unwind. The Rezzanine is an all inclusive space where anyone, no matter age, background, or gender, can feel comfortable and enjoy hours and hours of gaming bliss.

2. What are eSports?

ESports, short for electronic sports, is a form of competition using video games. Often it will take the form of organized multiplayer video game competitions, individually or as a team. In the last 15 years, eSports has seen a massive spike in popularity with the advent of streaming, so much so that most major dev studios now take into consideration the competitive scene as part of their design process
